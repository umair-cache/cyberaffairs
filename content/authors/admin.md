The CyberAffairs editorial team is made up of security researchers, penetration testers, and technology journalists dedicated to delivering accurate, timely, and accessible cybersecurity intelligence.

We believe that good security knowledge should be free and available to everyone — from CISOs and security engineers to curious individuals who simply want to stay safe online.

Our coverage spans the full threat landscape: vulnerability research, malware analysis, threat actor tracking, digital privacy, and security policy.
